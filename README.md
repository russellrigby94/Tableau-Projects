### ğŸµ Music & Audio Feature Analysis (Tableau)

Using Spotify audio feature data, I explored how musical attributes vary across genres and modes.

**Analysis highlights:**

* Popularity comparisons across music genres
* Danceability differences between **major and minor modes**
* Emotional tone (valence) trends by genre

**Key insights:**

* Pop and rap dominate in popularity, while niche genres rank lower
* Tracks in major mode tend to be more danceable
* Genres like reggae and ska show higher positive valence, while classical and soundtrack genres score lower
